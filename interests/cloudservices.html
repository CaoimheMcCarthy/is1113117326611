Cloud Services

The term 'cloud services' encapsulates a broad range of web-based technologies that can take the place of traditional methods in terms of storage, development enviroments and software. The primary types of Cloud Services are Software as a Service, Platform as a Service, Infrastructure as a Service and Communication as a Platform.
